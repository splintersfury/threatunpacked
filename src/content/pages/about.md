I try to approach my work with **ḥusn**—to do things with care, honesty, and intention, even when clarity is hard to reach.

This blog is a record of that effort: experiments, questions, half-finished ideas, and the slow process of understanding systems from the inside. Some things resist easy answers. Some paths feel impossible.

I write anyway, in the hope that the act of striving itself has value—and that, somewhere along the way, it helps me do things a little better.
